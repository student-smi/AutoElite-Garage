Our Story

About AutoElite

Founded in 2012 by master mechanic Rajesh Kumar, AutoElite began as a small workshop with a big mission: to bring dealership-quality service to independent garage pricing.

What started as a 3-bay workshop has grown into a 20-bay state-of-the-art facility serving over 15,000 vehicles annually. We expanded not just in size, but in capabilities — investing heavily in equipment, training, and technology to stay ahead of the rapidly evolving automotive landscape.

Today, AutoElite is the city's most trusted independent garage, known for our precision, transparency, and genuine care for every customer. We treat your car as if it were our own — because we know how much it matters to you.
